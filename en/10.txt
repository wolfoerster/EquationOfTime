In fact there are several definitions of "day" but if we see it with the eyes
of our ancestors, a day is the time between two consecutive noons.

So when exactly is noon? Is it at 12 o'clock in the daytime?
Well, for most people it would mean that, but solar noon doesn't care
about clocks and means the moment when the sun reaches exactly the
south direction.

To identify this moment we just have to draw a circle at the sky! 
The circle starts at the horizon in the south position, goes straight up
into the sky and right above our heads into the north direction.
