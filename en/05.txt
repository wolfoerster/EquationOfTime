We start the journey at the begin of spring, i.e. on March 20.
The sun is located at the center of our coordinate system and the earth
is located somewhere at the negative y axis, the spring-autumn axis (green).

The red x axis represents the winter-summer axis and the green disc in the 
xy plane is intended to emphasise this plane, which is called 'ecliptic'. 
The earth will never leave the ecliptic on its way around the sun.
